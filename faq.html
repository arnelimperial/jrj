HOW DO I MAKE A RESERVATION?

The best way to see if something is available is to call the cafe (716) 555-5555, and we'll be happy to make one for you. We do not take reservations by email. Sorry!

YOU TAKE WALK-INS?

Yes! And we even have a bar where you can forget your troubles while you wait to be seated.

DO YOU HAVE A WAIT-LIST?

Yes! There are no guarantees, but tables open up all the time due to cancellations and no-shows. Just give us a call (716) 555-5555 to get on the wait list.

CAN I CHANGE MY RESERVATION?

Absolutely. Just give us a call (716) 555-5555.

Do you accept deliveries? or what are your delivery options?

We don't have any form of delivery options as of now but you can call the cafe during regular hours to place your order for pickup.

WHERE ARE YOU? WHAT ARE YOUR HOURS? HOW DO I GET THERE?

All that info is available right here.

I HAVE DIETARY RESTRICTIONS. WHAT CAN YOU DO FOR ME?

We have detailed info right here but the short answer is:

Vegan? Just tell your server when you pay for your orders. All our dishes can be made vegan.

Gluten-free or Lactose-free? Just tell your server when you pay your orders. Some of our dishes can be prepared gluten-free. For buffet we have fewer options for gluten-free guests.

Allergies? Tell us when you make a reservation and we'll do our best to accommodate you. It's probably best to give us a call (716) 555-5555.

DO YOU SERVE ALCOHOL?

Yes, we serve wine.
